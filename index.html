About

Building systems and stories that scale.

Daniel Adewoye is a creative director and filmmaker whose work sits at the intersection of story, culture, and strategy — directing commercials, digital content, and campaigns built to perform in the real world.

Currently Head of Visuals at Glitch Africa, leading the studio's visual direction across original shows, editorial publishing, live sessions, and partner-led productions.

Founder of Dixtro and co-founder of Snappcode Academy — training 400+ creatives in content creation, photography, and visual storytelling.
